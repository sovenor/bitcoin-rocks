HOW TO PRINT & POST THESE BITCOIN FLYERS


Our mission is to help you orange pill more people by putting Bitcoin flyers in public. This flyer has a QR code that links to our educational Bitcoin website.

Print this flyer at home or at a print shop. Then, post it on bulletin boards, telephone poles around town, and other public spaces where people can see it and learn about Bitcoin.
