What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is two things: digital money and a computer network.

You can send bitcoin (the digital money) directly to other people using the Bitcoin Network.

The Bitcoin Network is able to operate without middlemen or central authorities, like banks or credit card companies, so you can avoid their transaction fees.

Bitcoin transactions achieve final settlement quickly (10 minutes) and can never be charged back, so you can sleep easy knowing your money is your money.

How can Bitcoin benefit my business?
