BITCOIN IS GOOD FOR YOU TOO!

Bitcoin doesn't have inflation

Inflation happens when more money is printed or created from thin air. This makes your money worth less over time.

Bitcoin has a fixed supply, which means no one can print more Bitcoin. Learn more.

Bitcoin doesn't have bank runs

Multiple US banks have collapsed in the past few years due to bank runs.

Instead of just holding your money for you, banks invest and lend out your money. If those investments don't do well, they don't have enough to pay you back.

And the FDIC insurance fund only has $1 for every $100 they're insuring. Learn more.

Bitcoin is permissionless

Unlike traditional financial networks, Bitcoin doesn't require permission to be used.

That means no one can stop you from using Bitcoin for any reason. It's the first financial network you can use without fear of censorship or seizure. Learn more.

Bitcoin is building a better world

Bitcoin is a misunderstood technology that is building a better world.

Bitcoin has enabled human rights activists to fight for freedom, reduced global methane emissions, saved national parks, and so much more. Learn more.
